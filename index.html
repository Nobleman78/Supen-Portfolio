Short Biography

Hi, I’m Supen Chakma, a passionate Civil Engineering graduate from the Chittagong University of Engineering & Technology (CUET). I have hands-on experience in both structural and geotechnical engineering, and I’ve worked on various projects, including residential building design using ETABS and AutoCAD. I’m proficient in engineering software such as PLAXIS 3D, SewerGEMS , HEC-HMS, and MS Project. With a strong interest in sustainable infrastructure and soil-structure interaction, I’m committed to developing innovative solutions through both academic research and practical fieldwork. My academic journey began at Tewfa Ideal School (SSC) and continued at Khagrachari Cantonment School and College (HSC), which laid a strong foundation for my career in engineering.
